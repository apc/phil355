Readings for 8/3

Nothing to read, but I recommend you listen to this episode of Radiolab.

Readings for 8/5

  • Descartes, excerpts from the Meditations on First Philosophy. (Chalmers)
  • Kim, ‘Introduction’, up until the end of the section titled ‘Varieties of Mental Phenomena’.

Readings for 8/10

  • Descartes, excerpts from The Passions of the Soul. (Chalmers).
  • Smullyan, ‘An Unfortunate Dualist’. (Chalmers)
  • Kim, chapter 6, read until the end of the section titled ‘Cartesian Interactionism’, skipping the section titled ‘Mental Causation, Mental Realism, Epiphenomenalism’.

Readings for 8/12

  • Ryle, ‘Descartes’ Myth’. (Chalmers)
  • Kim, chapter 2, up until the end of ‘Do Pains entail Pain Behavior?’.

Readings for 8/17

  • Putnam, ‘Brains and Behavior’. (Chalmers)
  • Kim, chapter 2, starting from ‘Ontological Behaviorism’ until the end of the chapter.

Readings for 8/19

  • Place, ‘Is Consciousness a Brain Process?’. (Chalmers)
  • Kim, chapter 3, up until the end of ‘What Does ’‘Identity’‘ Mean?’.

Readings for 8/24

  • Smart, ‘Sensations and Brain Processes’. (Chalmers)
  • Kim, chapter 3, section: ‘Token Physicalism and Type Physicalism’.

Readings for 8/26

  • Kripke, Saul A. Naming and Necessity, pp. 144–55.
  • Kim, chapter 3, section: ‘Objections to the Identity Theory’.
